Welcome to My Hometown: Dhaka

Capital city of Bangladesh

About Dhaka

Dhaka is a busy and colorful city known for its rich history, friendly people, and delicious food. It sits on the banks of the Buriganga River and is famous for rickshaws, old Mughal buildings, and lively markets.

Why I Like It

  • Warm and welcoming community
  • Tasty street food and sweets
  • Historic places to explore
  • Festivals and cultural events

Famous Places

  1. Lalbagh Fort
  2. Ahsan Manzil (Pink Palace)
  3. National Parliament Building
  4. Dhakeshwari Temple

Local Foods to Try

Biriyani, Kachchi, Fuchka, Jhalmuri, and Mishti (sweets).
